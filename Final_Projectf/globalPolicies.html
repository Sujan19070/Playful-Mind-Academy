Global Policies

1. Data Privacy & Protection Policy

Playful Academy ensures that all user data including student, teacher, and administrator information is handled with strict confidentiality.

Personal data must not be shared, sold, or accessed without proper authorization. Security measures must be followed at all times.

2. Acceptable Use Policy

The platform is intended strictly for educational purposes. Any misuse of system resources, unauthorized access, or malicious activities are prohibited.

Violations may lead to suspension or permanent termination of user access.

3. Content Standards Policy

All content shared on the platform must maintain academic integrity and professional standards.

Content containing hate speech, harassment, misinformation, or copyrighted material without permission is not allowed.

4. Audit & Accountability Policy

All administrative actions are recorded in the Audit Logs for transparency and accountability.

Audit records should only be accessed or modified by authorized administrators when required.
