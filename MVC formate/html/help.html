Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new cohort? +

To create a new cohort, go to the Cohorts page and click the "Create New Cohort" button. Fill in the required information including cohort name, grade level, and section. You can also assign a color theme for easy identification.

How can I assign an assignment to multiple cohorts? +

When creating an assignment, you'll see a "Assign to Cohorts" section with checkboxes. Simply select all the cohorts you want to assign the work to. You can also select "All Cohorts" to assign to everyone.

How do screen time policies work? +

Screen time policies allow you to set daily limits on device usage. You can configure different limits for weekdays and weekends, and set specific active hours. When a student reaches their limit, they'll receive a notification and access will be restricted.

Can I export progress reports? +

Yes! Go to the Cohort Progress page and click the "Export Report" button. You can choose to export data for specific cohorts, date ranges, and in various formats including PDF and Excel.

How do I handle parent approval requests? +

Parent approval requests appear in the Approvals section. You can review each request, see the details, and either approve or deny it. Parents will be notified of your decision automatically.
